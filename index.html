Skydive Pages

It is a management tool to for people involved in sport of Skydiving. It helps to log completed jumps, structure their details as location, aircraft, gear used, exit altitude, freefall time, as well as to associate pictures and videos taken during those jumps. It allows to share jump info/pictures/videos with other users. It is ongoing project and features like calendar of events, picture/video comments are currently in progress of development.
